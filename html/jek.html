My Heart Will Go On" is a song by Celine Dion, famously known as the theme song of the movie "Titanic." The song captures the enduring power of love and the strength to carry on even in the face of loss. Its meaning revolves around the idea that love transcends time and space, and though a loved one may be physically absent, their memory and the love shared will forever reside in the heart.
